# KovaaksTracker.exe

A tracker tool for KovaaK's stats and progress.
It shows play counts, levels, graphs, ranks, top percentages, and custom tiers in one place.

<img width="1094" height="673" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 103757" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8cb9e41e-3a48-415e-b81a-46091d57e461" />
First, click Select Stats Folder in the top-right corner.

<img width="762" height="478" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 104204" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f58b9c20-3d7f-4802-b3fa-a61be93dc549" />
Then open your KovaaK's local files, go to FPSAimTrainer, select the stats folder, and click Select Folder.

<img width="1639" height="622" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 104516" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dc07eca0-b94a-4b04-b44a-694f762aea29" />
If you do not know where KovaaK's local files are located, you can find them through Steam.

<img width="835" height="594" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 104537" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e1dc3a0b-a88b-4930-9eea-216acd3f0a1a" />
Click the gear icon on the right side, then go to Installed Files and click Browse.

<img width="631" height="128" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 104602" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ac747be1-8927-44fc-928f-c36fab002cf0" />
You should then see the FPSAimTrainer folder.

<img width="1270" height="673" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 104759" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fa224804-03e4-4157-b1c8-8adcb35f00f6" />
If the folder is selected successfully, the table will populate like this.


<img width="1271" height="672" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 105003" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e1fa9e71-e072-406f-8f69-2de834549291" />
After selecting any scenario, click Show Graph.

<img width="791" height="523" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 105044" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/180ddacc-1c1a-4c29-9ca2-07c3dc41d122" />
This will open a graph showing values such as your daily average score and daily max score.

<img width="1266" height="672" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 105246" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b9940eb6-3d46-4c0b-b1b1-0502bc7ff0ca" />
You can also click Sync Selected Scenario after selecting a scenario.

<img width="1268" height="673" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 105456" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1ebaad9e-78a9-4424-a733-ac1c49ee2c07" />
This will display the total number of leaderboard entries, your rank, your top percentage, and your custom tier used by this tool.
There is also a Sync All button next to it, but it is not recommended, since it can take a long time to finish.

<img width="1271" height="672" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 105754" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4be2e144-20a5-4c45-86ef-0d2fa5f61d39" />
You can also sort the table by play count, rank, or tier.

<img width="1274" height="674" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 110210" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/79d2b954-a7f6-43b6-ac2e-bd63ef049fed" />
Here, each scenario is assigned a level based on its play count.
The level increases by 1 for every 100 plays.
Please note that resets during a scenario are not counted as separate plays.

<img width="1273" height="673" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 110514" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/34c592a8-024b-41c5-aa8b-ab6af4bd89f2" />
The top section also shows your total number of scenario plays in KovaaK's and your corresponding overall level.

<img width="1267" height="674" alt="スクリーンショット 2026-03-16 110652" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3d7234f6-f663-474c-ae8a-47c2744b3f99" />
Click Refresh Local Data to reload your local stats data once.


Tiers are ordered from highest to lowest as follows:

・Legend

・Mythic

・Celestial

・Grandmaster

・Master

・Diamond

・Platinum

・Gold

・Silver

・Bronze

Tier criteria:

・Legend: Top 0.01%

・Mythic: Top 0.05%

・Celestial: Top 0.1%

・Grandmaster: Top 1%

・Master: Top 5%

・Diamond: Top 10%

・Platinum: Top 20%

・Gold: Top 35%

・Silver: Top 60%

・Bronze: Below that
